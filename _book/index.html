Preface

This project initially aimed to serve as the author’s personal resource of automatically updated financial data for guiding unbiased and premeditated investing decision. As the project went along, it has gradually progressed into a more public reference for the author’s close friends who are interested in investing and seek a financial data resource which is not exhausting but tidy and easy-to-get. All data presented on this project site are updated at least once a day. They were last updated at 2021-01-29 11:19:28 KST. This project has been hugely inspired by Warren Buffett, Town & Town (2018), Graham (2003), and 존리 (2020). This is a publicly accessible project still in its infant stage, and the author is always open to suggestions from whoever hopes to make it better. Please feel free to contact the author, or even better make a pull request!
